ramen recipe

description:

ramen is a great cheap savory meal, personally i love shin ramen!

ingredients

  1. ramen packet
  2. water

steps:

  1. put 2 cups of water in a pot to boil
  2. as it starts to boil, put the ramen and all the packets into the water, then mix occasionaly for 4 minutes
  3. put into a bowl, then enjoy!
