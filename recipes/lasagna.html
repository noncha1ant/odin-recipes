Lasagna recipe

description:

alright my guy, you know how to make lasagna. you just boil somw water, throw some lasagna pasta in there and cook it al dente. then make your tomato sauce and ground meat, then layer the lasagna pasta with sauce, meat and cheese, bake and enjoy!

ingredients:

you will need:

  1. olive oil
  2. tomatoes
  3. herbs and spices
  4. optional: wine
  5. ground meat (prefferably beef)
  6. mozzarella
  7. ricotta cheese
  8. parmesan cheese
  9. lasagna pasta

Steps:

  1. first, boil water in a pot, then add your pasta, and boil it until its only a bit undercooked. set aside.
  2. put some olive oil into a pot, after it heats up, add some ground meat, then cook it with some salt and pepper until it starts to brown heavily and leave fond on the pot, then promptly place it into a container.
  3. after removing the ground meat, quickly, before the fond burns, deglaze with some wine, or by adding half cut tomatoes, then add your herbs and spices.
  4. after deglazing, make sure to scrape the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon, NOT METAL or youll scratch the pot, and give it time to simmer.
  5. when done, preheat your oven and layer your lasagna
  6. bake your lasagna and youre done, enjoy!
